Academic Journey

I started my educational journey at a language-focused high school, where I immersed myself in English, Spanish, and French. This not only shaped my linguistic skills but also my ability to adapt to different cultures and perspectives.

I continued my studies with a Bachelor’s degree in Global Governance, an international and interdisciplinary program that opened my eyes to diverse fields like economics, politics, and technology. I specialized in Science and Technology, where I developed a keen interest in innovation and problem-solving.

Currently, I’m pursuing a Master’s degree in Cybersecurity. This exciting journey is helping me hone my skills in securing data and protecting digital infrastructures, aligning perfectly with my passion for creating a safer digital world where users can trust that their data is protected, their privacy is respected, and their online experiences remain secure.
